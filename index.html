Prompt Craft 101: The Art of Speaking to AI

Move beyond simple queries. Learn the structures and principles for crafting prompts that generate precise, creative, and actionable outputs from AI models. This foundational skill transforms AI from a novelty into a powerful co-pilot for your work and creativity.

Course Narrative

This micro-learning course is designed to transform your interactions with AI from basic question-answering to sophisticated co-creation. Through a series of progressive modules, you'll learn how to structure prompts that elicit high-quality, nuanced, and contextually appropriate responses from AI systems.

We begin by exploring the psychology of human-AI communication, establishing why precise language matters when interacting with large language models. You'll discover how AI interprets context, follows instructions, and generates responses based on subtle cues in your prompts.

The course progresses through practical applications across various domains - from creative writing and technical documentation to data analysis and problem-solving. Each module builds upon the previous, creating a comprehensive framework for AI communication mastery.

Learning Pathway

1
Foundation Principles
Understand the core concepts of tokens, context windows, and how AI models process language.
2
Prompt Structures
Learn different prompt frameworks: role-playing, chain-of-thought, and example-driven prompting.
3
Iterative Refinement
Master the art of analyzing AI outputs and refining your prompts for better results.
4
Advanced Techniques
Explore temperature settings, few-shot learning, and controlling output format and style.
